# Sound Effects for Stress Induction

Place the following sound files in this directory:

## Required Sound Files:

### 1. error_alarm.mp3
- **Purpose**: Critical error sound for visual and auditory induction
- **Description**: Loud alarm sound, like a system error or emergency alert
- **Duration**: ~3-4 seconds
- **Characteristics**: High pitch beeping, urgent tone
- **Example**: Similar to computer BIOS error beep or fire alarm

### 2. heartbeat.mp3
- **Purpose**: Rapid heartbeat sound for auditory induction
- **Description**: Fast, pounding heartbeat
- **Duration**: ~3-4 seconds
- **Characteristics**: 120+ BPM, tense and anxious feeling
- **Example**: Horror movie heartbeat sound effect

### 3. glitch.mp3
- **Purpose**: Digital glitch/static sound for cognitive induction
- **Description**: TV static, digital distortion, or radio interference
- **Duration**: ~3-4 seconds
- **Characteristics**: Crackling, buzzing, distorted digital noise
- **Example**: TV white noise with digital artifacts

## Where to Get Free Sound Effects:

1. **Freesound.org** (https://freesound.org)

2. **Zapsplat** (https://www.zapsplat.com)

## File Format:
- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128 kbps or higher
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz
- Mono or Stereo: Either is fine

## Volume Notes:
- Sounds should be LOUD but not painful
- Test at comfortable listening volume
- The code sets volume to 80% (0.8)
