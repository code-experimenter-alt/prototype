Music Privacy Study

A research study on music recommendation and user experience

What is this study about?

This study investigates how people interact with music recommendation systems and how physiological states affect recommendation privacy.

What will you do?

  • Complete a short survey about your music preferences (~5 minutes)
  • Participate in attention tasks with physiological monitoring (~30 minutes)
  • Browse music recommendations during different states
  • Provide feedback on your experience

Time Commitment

Approximately 35-45 minutes

Compensation

$15 gift card (or course credit)

Eligibility

  • Age 18 or older
  • Comfortable using web browsers
  • Access to headphones or speakers
  • Willing to wear a heart rate monitor (or use simulated data)
  • No hearing impairments that would affect music listening
