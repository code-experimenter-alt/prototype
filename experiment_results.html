Key Finding: RA+Mix Balances Privacy and Utility

Our results demonstrate that the RA+Mix approach (α=0.6) achieves near-optimal privacy protection (high overlap between stress/baseline recommendations) while maintaining acceptable recommendation utility.

📝 Research Conclusions

RQ1: Privacy Protection

RA+Mix significantly reduces exposure risk compared to Baseline-R. State-Agnostic provides maximum privacy but at the cost of personalization.

RQ2: Utility Preservation

Users report higher satisfaction with RA+Mix than State-Agnostic, while privacy concerns are significantly lower than Baseline-R.

RQ3: Stress Context

Heart rate monitoring confirms successful stress induction. Privacy preferences may shift under stress, making protection more critical.
