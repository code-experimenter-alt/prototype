Informed Consent

Purpose of the Study

You are invited to participate in a research study about music recommendation systems. The purpose is to understand how users interact with different types of recommendations and how these systems might reveal information about user states.

What You Will Do

If you agree to participate, you will:

  • Complete questionnaires about your music preferences and current mood
  • Complete brief tasks (some may involve mild mental challenge)
  • Explore and listen to music recommendations using our web interface
  • Provide feedback about your experience

Time Commitment

The study will take approximately 45-60 minutes to complete.

Risks and Benefits

Risks: Some tasks may cause mild, temporary stress similar to everyday experiences (e.g., mental arithmetic under time pressure). You can stop at any time if you feel uncomfortable. There are no other anticipated risks.

Benefits: You will contribute to scientific understanding of recommendation systems. You will receive $15 compensation (or equivalent course credit).

Confidentiality

Your participation is completely confidential. We will not collect any personally identifiable information beyond basic demographics. All data will be stored securely and identified only by a random participant code. Data may be used in published research, but no information will identify you personally.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may skip any questions or withdraw from the study at any time without penalty. If you withdraw, your data will be deleted upon request.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this study, please contact [Researcher Name] at [Email]. If you have questions about your rights as a research participant, contact the [University] IRB at [IRB Contact].

Consent

  • You agree to participate in this research study
