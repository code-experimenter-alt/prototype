⚠️ Important Information

Attention Task: You will complete a brief attention test where numbers appear on screen. Press the spacebar (or click the button) when you see the number 7.

Note: During the task, system alerts may appear. Please respond naturally to any alerts that occur.

Please ensure:

  • ✅ You are in a quiet environment with minimal distractions
  • ✅ You can wear headphones or have speakers available
  • ✅ You will remain seated during the entire session
  • ✅ Your heart rate device is connected (if using real device)
  • ✅ You complete all 3 sessions in one sitting

📱 Content Browsing

During the heart rate monitoring phase, you will browse personalized content recommendations including:

  • Music tracks
  • Mindfulness exercises
  • Productivity tools
  • Wellness content

You can click on items that interest you. The system will personalize recommendations based on your preferences.
